CS-MathMate

A modern mathematical suite built with Flutter.

Product Overview

CS-MathMate is a modern mathematical suite built with Flutter for high-performance support (Windows). Powered by an offline-first AST-based symbolic engine, CS-MathMate provides detailed step-by-step mathematical logic and reduction steps for educational verification, assisting students with advanced mathematics including Calculus, Linear Algebra, and Statistics.

Key Solvers & Modules

✦

Trigonometry

Solve angles, trigonometric functions, ratios, and algebraic trigonometric identity equations step-by-step.

✦

Probability & Statistics

Calculate combinations, permutations, analyze datasets for mean, median, standard deviations, and compute complex probability distributions.

✦

Number Theory

Compute divisibility, find prime factors, calculate LCM/GCD, evaluate modular arithmetic, and solve sequence progressions.

✦

Sets & Relations

Evaluate set unions, intersections, differences, Cartesian products, and classify properties of logical relations.
